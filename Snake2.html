Tutorial

• Move with your selected control scheme (Arrow Keys by default).
• Eat apples for Gold + XP. Every level gives +1 SP and higher XP scaling.
• Spend SP in Permanent Skills to increase Speed, Start Size, and Growth.
• Soul powers unlock at Level 10. Rebirth grants shards for permanent upgrades.
• Buffs are run-only, but skins/upgrades persist between runs.
• Press your Phase key (Space by default) after unlocking Phase Shift.
• Save/Load with Export/Import code to keep progress safe.
